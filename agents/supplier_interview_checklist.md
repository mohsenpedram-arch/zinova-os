# ZINOVA – Supplier Interview Checklist (WeChat / Call)

## Section 1 – Basic Identity
- [ ] Company full name (as on business license)
- [ ] Factory address (city, district, street)
- [ ] Year founded
- [ ] Number of employees
- [ ] Main product lines (EV charging? IC‑CPD only?)

## Section 2 – Manufacturing
- [ ] Factory area (m²)
- [ ] Monthly production capacity (units)
- [ ] Own production line for IC‑CPD? (photo/video requested)
- [ ] QC department size and test equipment (Hi‑Pot, leakage tester, thermal chamber)
- [ ] Do you have ISO9001? (certificate)
- [ ] Export markets (EU, Middle East, Southeast Asia?)

## Section 3 – Certification & Engineering
- [ ] IEC62752 certificate number and issuing lab (TUV, SGS, etc.)
- [ ] Do you have 6mA DC leakage detection? (Type B or dedicated circuit)
- [ ] Can you share the RCD block diagram?
- [ ] Do you design your own PCB? Or ODM from third party?
- [ ] Do you write your own firmware? (for ICCB)
- [ ] Can you adjust current levels (6/8/10/13/16A) via button?
- [ ] Do you have plug temperature sensor?

## Section 4 – OEM / ODM Capability
- [ ] Can you put Zinova logo and Nomad model name?
- [ ] Minimum order quantity for private label?
- [ ] Sample lead time? Cost for sample?
- [ ] Do you provide factory test report for each unit?
- [ ] Warranty period? Spare parts policy?

## Section 5 – Commercial
- [ ] MOQ for first order (units)
- [ ] Sample price (FOB, EXW)
- [ ] Mass production price (at 500pcs, 1000pcs)
- [ ] Payment terms (T/T, L/C, etc.)
- [ ] Lead time for 100 units, 500 units

## Post‑Interview Scoring
- Green (pass): ≥ 8 positive signals
- Yellow (discuss): 5–7 signals, need more evidence
- Red (reject): ≤ 4 signals or any hard rejection rule
