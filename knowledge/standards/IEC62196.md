# IEC 62196-2 – Plugs, socket-outlets, vehicle connectors and vehicle inlets – AC

## Scope
Dimensional compatibility and safety requirements for AC connectors used for EV charging.

## Relevant part for Nomad
**Type 2 female connector** (also called "Mennekes" type). Characteristics:
- 7 pins: 3 phases + neutral + ground + 2 signal pins (CP, PP).
- Rated up to 480 V AC, 63 A (but Nomad uses single‑phase 16 A).
- Shutter mechanism for safety.
- Mechanical lock optional.

## PP resistor coding (IEC 61851‑1 + 62196‑2)
The **proximity pilot (PP)** resistor inside the vehicle connector tells the IC‑CPD the cable current rating. Values:
- 13 A → 680 Ω
- 20 A → 220 Ω (used for 16 A)
- 32 A → 100 Ω

Nomad requires PP reading to verify cable capacity.

## Critical for sourcing
- Must be genuine Type 2 (not modified Chinese version).
- Temperature sensor inside connector preferred for safety.
- Vendor must provide mating cycle test data (≥10,000 cycles).

## Red flags
- Connector without CE marking.
- No PP pin or resistor coding.
- No datasheet for the specific connector model.
