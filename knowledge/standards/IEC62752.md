# IEC 62752 – In‑Cable Control and Protection Device (IC‑CPD) for Mode 2 charging

## Scope
Product standard for portable EV charging cables that plug into a standard socket (Mode 2). This is **the core standard for Zinova Nomad**.

## Mandatory features (from standard)
1. **Residual current protection**
   - AC leakage: Type A (30 mA) equivalent.
   - **DC leakage**: 6 mA smooth DC detection (mandatory).
2. **Ground continuity monitoring** – no earth = no start.
3. **Overcurrent protection** (16 A).
4. **Over‑/under‑voltage protection** (207–253 V range).
5. **Temperature monitoring** – plug and control box.
6. **Pilot (CP) signal** according to IEC 61851‑1.
7. **PP resistor recognition**.
8. **Automatic restart prohibition** after critical fault (leakage, over‑temp).

## Certification requirements
- Full type test by an accredited lab (TÜV, SGS, Intertek, etc.).
- Test report must include the 6 mA DC leakage test.
- No self‑declaration accepted.

## Critical for Zinova Agent
- Ask for **certificate number** and **test report**.
- Verify that the report explicitly mentions “6 mA smooth DC leakage detection”.
- If vendor says “IEC 62752 equivalent” or “according to IEC 62752” without certificate → **reject**.

## Common fake claims
- “We follow IEC 62752 internally” – not sufficient.
- “Our customers never asked for certificate” – unacceptable.
- “We can get it later” – reject until certificate provided.
